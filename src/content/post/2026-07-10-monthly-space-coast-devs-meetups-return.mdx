Monthly Space Coast Devs meetups are starting back up, and we are keeping them casual.

Join us every third Wednesday at Protowork Studio Hangout from 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM. The first hour is open networking and catching up with other people in the local tech community. The second hour is for a light presentation or two, community lightning talks, tech-topic discussion, or sharing updates on projects people are building.

- **When:** Every third Wednesday, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- **Where:** Protowork Studio Hangout
- **Format:** Casual networking from 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM, then presentations, lightning talks, or project updates from 7:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- **Who should come:** Developers, designers, founders, IT professionals, students, makers, and anyone interested in technology on the Space Coast

No polished talk required. Bring a project update, a question, something you learned recently, or just yourself.

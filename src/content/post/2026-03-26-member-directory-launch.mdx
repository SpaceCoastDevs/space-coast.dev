We've been building something behind the scenes — and today we're excited to launch the **Space Coast Devs Member Directory**.

## What It Is

The member directory is a place to discover who's in our community. Whether you're a developer, designer, data scientist, IT professional, or maker — if you're part of Space Coast Devs, you can now have a public profile that shows off your skills and connects you with others.

## How to Join

Signing up takes less than a minute:

1. Click **Sign In** in the top-right corner of the site
2. Log in with your Discord account
3. Verify your email address
4. Fill out your profile — add your bio, location, skills, and social links
5. Set your profile to **public** and you'll appear in the directory

## Skills

We've put together a broad skills catalog designed for the full range of talents in our community, including:

- **Languages, Frontend, Backend, and Mobile** — for the developers
- **Design & UX** — for designers and creatives
- **Science & Data** — for the data folks and scientists
- **IT & Sysadmin** — for IT professionals
- **Cyber Security** — for the security-minded
- **Amateur Radio** — for the hams in our ranks
- **Maker & Fabrication** — for the 3D printers, PCB designers, and tinkerers
- **Gaming** — for game developers and graphics engineers
- **Community & Leadership** — for mentors, speakers, and open source contributors

Can't find a skill that fits? We'll be expanding the catalog based on community feedback — reach out on Discord and let us know what's missing.

## Privacy Is Your Choice

Your profile is **private by default**. You choose when to make it public. Only your display name and avatar are pulled from Discord — everything else (bio, location, skills, social links) is entirely up to you.

## Come Introduce Yourself

Once your profile is live, swing by the [#introductions](https://discord.com/channels/1024081514260078603/1034860347951153192) channel on Discord and share your member link. We'd love to know who's in the community.
